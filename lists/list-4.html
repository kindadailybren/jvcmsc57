IV. DISCRETE PROBABILITY

Discrete Probability was definitely the most fun topic for me out of all the topics we covered. It was packed with problem-solving, which I personally enjoy a lot. What made it even more interesting were the real-life scenarios we got to analyze, like making decisions under uncertainty, calculating odds, and thinking in probabilities. As a Dota player, I could relate it to calculating odds of crits or winning probability. One memorable experience was when my friends and I played a gambling card game called "In-Between" during our trip to Samal — spoiler alert: I lost. But honestly, applying what we learned to that game made it even more entertaining and helped the concepts stick. Probability isn't just math, it's everywhere in life, games, and even risks we take.
